About

Who

Is ARC? we are the Athletics & Recreation Center. This is an exciting venture for our algonquin community. This facility will provide our students with a place to break through perceptual barriers in a welcoming, safe, inclusive and inviting enviroment for students of all backgrounds, cultures, and ability to spark a movement

What

is this going cost to me? This facility will be included in your activity fees. All fee-paying student can use and partcipate in all activities in the building. As for the facility, it will cost Algonquin College $48.8million.

where

will the ARC building be? The ARC building will be located northeast of the student common building, opposite of the residence. The facility will be an estimated 100,000 square feet! Connected to the student Commons by a "plus15" Pedestrain link.
